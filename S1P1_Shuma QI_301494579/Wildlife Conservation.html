Focus on Endangered Species

Many species are on the brink of extinction due to habitat destruction, climate change, and poaching. Here are a few critically endangered species we focus on:

  • African Elephants: Threatened by poaching and habitat loss.
  • Sea Turtles: Endangered due to plastic pollution and fishing nets.
  • Tigers: Facing habitat loss and illegal wildlife trade.
  • Orangutans: Affected by deforestation for palm oil plantations.

The Comeback of the Bald Eagle

Through dedicated conservation efforts, sea otters are making a remarkable recovery along the California coast, helping restore kelp forest ecosystems.

Rhino Sanctuary Success

In Kenya, rhino sanctuaries are working tirelessly to protect endangered black and white rhinos from poaching, ensuring their survival for future generations.
