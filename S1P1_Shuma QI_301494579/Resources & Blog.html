Understanding Animal Welfare Frameworks

Animal welfare laws and frameworks ensure humane treatment of animals in research, exhibitions, and farming. These policies aim to uphold the Five Freedoms: freedom from hunger, discomfort, pain, distress, and to express natural behavior.

How Policies Shape Animal Protection

National governments and organizations like the WOAH play a crucial role in enforcing laws and creating educational programs that promote animal-friendly policies.
